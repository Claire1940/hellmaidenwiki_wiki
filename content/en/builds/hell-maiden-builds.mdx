<Section icon={Map} title="Core Deck-Building Mechanics in Hell Maiden">

The foundation of all successful **hell maiden builds** is a deep understanding of the card-based upgrade system. Unlike traditional survivors where upgrades are random stat boosts, this game utilizes a physical deck where the position of your cards determines their effectiveness. You can hold up to four unique weapon cards, each modified by a stack of mod cards.

Understanding the rarity and type of cards you encounter is vital for long-term survival in the Circles of Hell.

| Card Type | Primary Function | Strategic Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Weapon Card** | The base attack/spell | **Core** of the build; dictates playstyle |
| **Boost Card** | Increases a single stat | **High** for specialized damage scaling |
| **Effect Card** | Adds status (Burn/Slow) | **Medium** for crowd control needs |
| **Spread Card** | Shares buffs to neighbors | **Excellent** for multi-weapon synergy |
| **Miracle Card** | Multiple powerful boosts | **Top-Tier** rarity; always priority |
| **Curse Card** | High power at a cost | **Situational**; high-risk, high-reward |

<Callout type="tip" title="Positioning Matters">
Always check if your Mod Card has a directional requirement. Some cards only boost weapons to the left or right, making the order of your deck just as important as the cards themselves.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="The 'Chain Lightning' Speed Build">

The **Chain Lightning** build focuses on high attack speed and screen-wide clearing. By utilizing the **Wayfinder** signature weapon, which shoots swerving projectiles, you can trigger the **I of Love** mod card consistently. This build excels at clearing the "Limbo" level where enemies tend to swarm in massive, low-health packs.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Required Weapons">
    - **Wayfinder**: Main projectile source.
    - **Lightning Bolt**: Passive AoE strikes.
    - **Blazing Quill**: For close-range defense.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Key Mod Synergies">
    - **I of Love**: 80% chance for chain lightning at Level 3.
    - **Swift Strikes**: Negates attack speed penalties.
    - **Invigorate**: Massive 300% damage boost.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

To maximize this build, you must manage the trade-off between damage and speed. The **Invigorate** card provides a massive 200% to 300% damage increase but significantly slows your attack speed. Pairing it with **Swift Strikes** is the only way to maintain the high-frequency procs needed for the lightning chains to clear the screen.

| Build Component | Level 1 Effect | Level 3 Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **I of Love Proc** | 30% Chain Chance | **80% Chain Chance** |
| **Lightning Damage** | Base Strike | **+300% Total Damage** |
| **Chain Targets** | 6 Enemies | **10+ Enemies** |
| **Attack Size** | Standard | **+60% Area Coverage** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Build Weakness">
This build is glass-cannon oriented. Without healing mods, you must rely on **Dash Distance** and **Movement Speed** charms to avoid being boxed in by elite enemies.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The 'Unkillable Moon' Sustain Build">

For players struggling with the boss fights in the second Circle of Hell, the **Unkillable Moon** build is the premier choice among all **hell maiden builds**. It centers on the **Selenic Bloom** weapon, which creates a lingering AoE ring of stars. When combined with life-steal mechanics, this build allows you to stand your ground even against the most aggressive fiends.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Selenic Bloom">
    Unlock this weapon by rescuing the Poet **Horace**. It serves as the anchor for your healing zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Stack Eternal Prestige">
    Apply the **Eternal Prestige** mod card to the moon. At Level 3, it provides a 15% chance to absorb HP on every hit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Expand the Orbit">
    Use **Heroic Surge** and **Attack Size** mods to make the moon larger. The more enemies inside the ring, the faster you heal.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Add Projectile Count">
    Level up your projectile mods to throw out 3 or 4 moons simultaneously, creating multiple overlapping healing zones.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Synergy Pair | Resulting Effect | Combat Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Moon + HP Regen** | Constant health recovery | **Excellent** for face-tanking bosses |
| **Moon + Size Up** | Massive zone control | **Good** for protecting NPCs/Objectives |
| **Moon + Projectile** | Multiple star rings | **High** DPS and healing frequency |

<Callout type="success" title="Sustainability Peak">
Once you reach Level 3 with **Eternal Prestige**, you can effectively ignore minor projectile damage as long as you are actively hitting a pack of enemies with your moons.
</Callout>

Early game focus should always be on **Experience Gain**. Charms that increase your XP from orbs by 14% to 28% are mandatory for reaching the high levels (Level 50+) required to fully max out your deck. Without these, you will struggle to find enough cards to merge your mods to Level 3.

| Milestone Time | Target Level | Build Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0-5 Minutes** | Level 15 | 2 Weapons, XP Charms active |
| **5-10 Minutes** | Level 30 | 1 Level 3 Mod, 3 Weapons |
| **10-15 Minutes** | Level 45 | Full Deck, Multiple Level 3 Mods |
| **15+ Minutes** | Level 55+ | Endgame scaling, Spell tokens ready |

<Callout type="tip" title="Spell Token Management">
Save your **Dante's Inferno** or other ultimate spells for when the screen is completely filled with elite "Eyeball" enemies. These spells provide temporary invulnerability and massive screen-clearing damage.
</Callout>
