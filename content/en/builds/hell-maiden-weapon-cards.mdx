<Section icon={Target} title="How to Optimize Your Card Deck">

Maximizing the potential of your **hell maiden weapon cards** requires a step-by-step approach to deck management. You cannot simply pick cards at random; you must organize them to take advantage of adjacency bonuses and level scaling.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Foundation">
    Choose a primary weapon card that matches your intended build. For 2026 meta, **Selenic Bloom** is highly recommended for its massive AoE potential.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Slot Critical Passives">
    Immediately look for **Venus Flavor**. Place it in a slot where it can affect adjacent weapons. This multiplies the value of a single card across your entire deck.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate the Merge">
    Once you have two identical cards of the same level, use the Merge function. A Level 3 card is significantly more powerful than three Level 1 cards combined.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Balance with Charms">
    Use the Charm slots for global buffs like **Movement Speed** or **Magnetic Pull**. These don't take up weapon slots but provide essential survival utility.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Adjacency Master">
By placing a Level 3 Venus Flavor card between two projectile weapons, you effectively gain +8 projectiles for the cost of one passive slot. This is the most efficient way to scale in the late game.
</Callout>

</Section>
