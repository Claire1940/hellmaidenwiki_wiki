### The Anatomy of a Spell Loadout

Every run in Hell Maiden starts with a signature weapon, but it evolves through the collection of Spirit Cards. These cards are divided into two primary categories that form the basis of your combat power.

| Card Type | Primary Function | Scaling Mechanic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Weapon Card** | Defines the base attack (Projectile, Melee, Area) | Level 1-3 upgrades through duplicates |
| **Mod Card** | Adds properties like Burn, Speed, or Multi-shot | Merging duplicates to Level 3 |
| **Poet Spell** | Screen-clearing ultimate with a cooldown | Unlocked via Poet rescue and progression |
| **Blessing** | Permanent passive buffs from rescued Poets | Meta-progression via the Forum |

Achieving the "Perfect Hand" achievement requires careful planning and a bit of luck with card draws. Follow these steps to maximize your **hell maiden spell combinations** during a standard 12-minute run in Limbo or Lust.

  <Step num="1" title="Secure Your Core Weapon">

  <Step num="2" title="Fill the Weapon Slots">

  <Step num="3" title="Merge Duplicate Mods">

  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Positional Layout">
    Open your deck menu and rearrange your cards. Place "Adjacent Buff" Mods next to your highest-damage Weapon to ensure it receives every possible multiplier.

  </Step>
