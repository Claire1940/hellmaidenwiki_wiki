<Section icon={Map} title="Optimal Card Layout and Positioning">

In Hell Maiden, where you place your cards is just as important as which cards you pick. The **hell maiden blazing quill build** benefits immensely from "modifier sharing," where one card boosts multiple weapons.

### The Adjacency Strategy
Using cards like **Venus's Favor** or **Dextral Charge** requires careful planning of your inventory grid. You want your highest-impact weapons to sit directly next to these boosters.

| Position | Content | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Slot 1** | Blazing Quill | Primary damage source. |
| **Slot 2** | Venus's Favor | Boosts projectiles for Slot 1 and 3. |
| **Slot 3** | Kindled Embers | Secondary fire support. |
| **Slot 4** | Dextral Charge | Speeds up weapons to the right. |

By placing **Venus's Favor** between the Quill and another weapon, you effectively double its value. This positioning allows you to fire multiple Blazing Quills at once, which, when combined with Level 3 **Sataris Path**, results in a screen-clearing wave of fire.

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Positioning">
If you find **Dextral Charge**, move your Blazing Quill to the right side of it. This ensures that even if you have heavy speed penalties from damage cards, the Dextral Charge will help keep your fire rate competitive.
</Callout>

</Section>
