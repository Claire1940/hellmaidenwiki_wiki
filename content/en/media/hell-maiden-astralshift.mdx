<Section icon={Swords} title="hell maiden astralshift Core Mechanics">

Developed by AstralShift, **hell maiden astralshift** combines fast-paced horde-survival action with a deep, tactical deck-building system inspired by Dante's Divine Comedy. Players control Dante as she descends through the Circles of Hell, her attacks firing automatically while players manually aim with the cursor or controller stick. This split-control scheme creates a high-skill ceiling where positioning, kiting, and active dodging are vital to surviving the punishing bullet-hell waves.

Dashing serves as your primary defensive tool, granting brief invulnerability frames to cross dangerous projectile lines or escape tight corners. As enemies fall, they drop experience orbs that trigger level-up choices. These choices allow you to draft new Weapon Cards, attach Mod Cards, or upgrade existing abilities in your hand. Managing your active hand is crucial, as you are limited to four Weapon slots and must carefully arrange your cards to benefit from positional synergies.

| Mechanic | Input / System | Tactical Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Movement | WASD / Left Stick | Constantly reposition to avoid contact damage and herd waves |
| Aiming | Mouse / Right Stick | Direct automatic attacks toward high-density groups or elites |
| Dashing | Spacebar / Face Button | Move through enemy lines safely; avoid bullet-hell patterns |
| Leveling | Experience Orbs | Level up mid-run to draft new Weapon Cards or Mod upgrades |

<Callout type="warning" title="Dash Conservation">
Do not use your dash for general movement. Always keep at least one charge available to escape surrounding hordes or dodge telegraphed boss attacks.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Best Spirit Cards & Build Setups">

Spirit Cards in **hell maiden astralshift** are divided into Weapon Cards and Mod Cards. Weapon Cards define your attack patterns, while Mod Cards enhance those weapons with utility, speed, and damage multipliers. A dependable build concentrates its strongest upgrades on one signature Weapon, using the remaining slots for complementary attacks, and merges duplicate Mods toward Level 3 instead of collecting many weak effects.

To maximize your damage output, pay close attention to card positioning. Some Mod Cards offer substantial bonuses to adjacent weapons or weapons placed to their right. Rearranging your hand dynamically during a run ensures your primary damage dealer receives all active positional buffs.

<Section icon={Map} title="Limbo and Lust Circle Walkthrough">

The Early Access build of **hell maiden astralshift** features two complete Circles of Hell: Limbo (Circle 1) and Lust (Circle 2). Both stages require you to survive a grueling 12-minute wave sequence before transitioning to their final boss encounters. While surviving, you must explore the map to locate Temples, complete optional quests, and rescue trapped Poets.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master Circle 1: Limbo">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Circle 2: Lust">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conquer Circle 2: Lust">
    Lust features highly aggressive enemies like Succubi and Vipers. Prioritize crowd control and status effects early to manage dense waves, rescue Ovid to expand your card pool, and prepare for the dual boss fight against Scarmiglione and Libicocco.
  </Step>
</Steps>
