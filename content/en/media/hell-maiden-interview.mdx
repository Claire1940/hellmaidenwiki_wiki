A significant portion of the **hell maiden interview** focused on the design philosophy behind the "Maidens" themselves. For the 2026 roster, the team is moving away from traditional class archetypes. Instead, they are focusing on "Hybrid Identities" that allow players to switch roles dynamically based on their equipped **Soul Relics**.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Soul Link Mechanics Overhaul">

Perhaps the most technical segment of the **hell maiden interview** involved the discussion of the **Soul Link** system. This mechanic, which allows two Maidens to share passive bonuses and ultimate abilities, is receiving a total overhaul to improve tactical depth in high-level play.

The developers admitted that the previous version of Soul Link was too restrictive, often forcing players into "Meta Duos" that stifled creativity. The 2026 update introduces the **Resonance Matrix**, a new skill tree dedicated entirely to the bond between your active characters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize the Resonance Matrix">
    Access the Matrix via the main character menu after reaching Level 30. This unlocks the first tier of shared passives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Primary Bond">
    Choose two Maidens to form a "Core Bond." This selection determines the baseline elemental affinity of your team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Distribute Resonance Points">
    Earn points through Abyss Raids to unlock nodes in the Matrix. Focus on nodes that complement both characters' strengths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Synergistic Ultimates">
    Once the Matrix is 50% complete, you can trigger "Dual Ultimates" during combat, combining the powers of both Maidens.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Requirement">
Breaking a Core Bond resets the current Resonance Matrix progress for those specific characters. Choose your pairings wisely to avoid wasting valuable resources.
</Callout>

| Shared Fortitude | Both Maidens share 20% of total HP | 10 Points |
| Rapid Swap | Reduces character swap cooldown by 2s | 15 Points |
