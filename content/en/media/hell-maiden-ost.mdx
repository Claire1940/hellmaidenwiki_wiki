<Section icon={Target} title="Using Audio Cues for Survival">

Mastering the **Hell Maiden OST** and its associated sound effects is vital for players aiming to reach **Level 50** and unlock the **Infernal Prowess I** achievement. The game uses specific audio triggers to warn players of off-screen threats and gameplay milestones.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Elite Spawns">
    Listen for a distinct, metallic clang or a unique roar. This signals the arrival of an **Elite Viper** or **Elite Succubus**. Moving away from the dense horde when this sound plays allows you to isolate the priority target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Track Spell Readiness">
    When your Poet's Spell (like **Dante's Inferno** or **Star Sign ☆ Pleiades**) is fully charged, a shimmering sound effect plays. This is your cue to save the attack for a crowded moment or a boss phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Monitor Health Thresholds">
    As your HP drops, the music becomes muffled and a heartbeat sound effect intensifies. This is a critical warning to prioritize survival and avoid contact damage, especially when pursuing the **Vital Endurance** achievements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Listen for Temple Activation">
    Activating a Temple provides a resonant, bell-like sound. If you are aiming for the **Pious Pilgrim** achievement (12 Temples in one run), use these audio confirmations to ensure you don't miss a nearby interaction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
In **Circle 2: Lust**, the **Lust Mines** have a very short audio fuse. If you hear a high-pitched hiss, dash immediately to avoid massive area-of-effect damage.
</Callout>

</Section>
