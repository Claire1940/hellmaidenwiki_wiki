<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Gameplay & Card Deckbuilding Mechanics">

At its core, the game is a unique blend of bullet heaven action and deckbuilding strategy. Unlike traditional survivors where upgrades are entirely passive, players here manage a hand of active weapon cards and modifying cards. Your hand can hold up to four unique weapon cards per run, which can be enhanced dynamically using various mod cards collected throughout the circles of hell.

Understanding how to position these cards in your hand is the key to maximizing your damage output and survivability. Mod cards are divided into five distinct types, each represented by a unique background color to help players make quick tactical decisions during intense combat.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Boost Cards">
    - **Single Stat Upgrades**
    - Increases specific attributes like damage or attack speed on an individual weapon card.
    - Ideal for hyper-focusing on your primary damage-dealing weapon.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Effect Cards">
    - **Status Ailments**
    - Applies extra status effects such as burn or weaken to your weapon attacks.
    - Essential for setting up high-damage elemental synergies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Spread Cards">
    - **Multi-Weapon Buffs**
    - Spreads boosts or status effects across multiple adjacent weapon cards in your hand.
    - Positioning is critical to maximize the reach of these cards.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Miracle & Curse Cards">
    - **High-Risk Modifiers**
    - Miracle cards grant multiple powerful boosts, while Curse cards offer massive advantages at a significant cost.
    - Best reserved for advanced builds that can offset the negative penalties.
  </Card>

To build an efficient deck, players must balance their offensive capabilities with defensive utilities. Combining weapons that cover different ranges ensures you are never overwhelmed by fast-moving melee units or long-range projectile threats.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Character Progression & Run Guide">

Surviving the depths of Limbo requires more than quick reflexes; players must follow a structured progression path during each run to build momentum before facing the ultimate challenge of the zone. The meta-progression loop rewards players who explore the map, complete side objectives, and systematically upgrade their active deck.

Rescuing lost allies like Horace during your runs unlocks unique blessings that can completely alter your tactical approach. Follow these essential steps to optimize your progression during a standard run.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Signature Weapon">
    Choose a starting weapon that aligns with your preferred playstyle. The Wayfinder is excellent for players who prefer active aiming, while Kindled Embers offers a safer, passive defensive zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage and Merge Your Hand">
    As you collect experience orbs, prioritize merging duplicate cards to free up hand space and boost your active attributes. Keep your most powerful weapons in slots that benefit from adjacent spread cards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Zone Quests">
    Keep an eye on the mini-map for unique quest markers, such as defending Horace. Completing these objectives grants powerful character blessings that persist for the remainder of the run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat the Underworld Boss">
    Prepare your deck for high single-target damage before confronting Queen Minos. Ensure you have adequate movement speed or defensive charms to dodge her high-damage judgment beams.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The characters you encounter throughout your journey provide both narrative depth and mechanical advantages. Building relationships and understanding their roles in the ongoing story will unlock further progression options.
