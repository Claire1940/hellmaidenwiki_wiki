<Section icon={Map} title="How to Unlock Voiced Dialogue and Story Chapters">

Unlocking the full vocal performances of the **hell maiden voice actors** requires active exploration and specific gameplay achievements. Because the narrative is tied directly to your progression through the Circles of Hell, you must balance surviving the 12-minute horde waves with seeking out the trapped Poets of Limbo.

Follow these steps to systematically unlock all voiced dialogue and story chapters in the current Early Access build:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Rescue the Poets in Their Respective Circles">
    To hear the voices of Horace, Homer, and Ovid, you must first locate and rescue them during your runs. Horace and Homer are trapped in Circle 1 (Limbo), while Ovid must be saved during your descent into Circle 2 (Lust).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit the Poets' Forum Between Runs">
    After a successful run or a tragic demise, return to the Poets' Forum. This hub area is where the rescued spirits gather. Look for speech bubble indicators above their heads to initiate fully voiced conversations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Exhaust All Dialogue Trees">
    Do not leave a conversation early. Keep interacting with each character until their dialogue loops. Exhausting these interactions is required to progress their personal questlines and unlock their signature Spells.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete Circle Challenges to Unlock Story Chapters">
    Major story chapters, such as Queen Minos's introductory sequences or the Malebranche encounters, require you to defeat bosses multiple times. For example, defeating Queen Minos three times unlocks the "Taboo Arts I" dialogue path.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Dialogue Reset Warning">
Dialogue progress in the Poets' Forum is saved permanently, but entering a new Circle before exhausting current conversations can sometimes delay specific character interactions until the next run.
</Callout>
