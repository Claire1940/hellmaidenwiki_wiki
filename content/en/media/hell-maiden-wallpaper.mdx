<Section icon={Target} title="How to Setup Your Hell Maiden Wallpaper">

To ensure your **hell maiden wallpaper** looks its best, you should follow a few technical steps. High-resolution art can sometimes lose detail if not scaled correctly, especially when dealing with the intricate "shoujo anime ornamentation" found in AstralShift's designs.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate High-Res Assets">
    Visit the Hell Maiden Steam Store page or the official AstralShift website. Right-click on the "Hero" or "Background" images to save them in their original resolution.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Your Resolution">
    Identify your monitor's native resolution (e.g., 1920x1080 or 3840x2160). If the image is smaller than your resolution, it may appear blurry.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Scaling Settings">
    In your OS settings, use the "Fill" or "Fit" option. Avoid "Stretch" as it will distort the symmetrical sigils and character proportions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Match System Accents">
    For a truly immersive look, set your Windows or macOS accent color to a "Crimson" or "Burgundy" hex code (like #E11D48) to match the game's UI.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resolution Warning">
Avoid using low-resolution thumbnails for your wallpaper. Always seek out the "Static" or "Header" links from the Steam CDN for the highest pixel density.
</Callout>
