<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Combat and Card Merging Mechanics">

At the heart of the **hell maiden demo** lies a sophisticated card system that dictates your offensive and defensive capabilities. Instead of simple level-up choices, players collect cards that represent specific attacks or buffs. The most critical mechanic to master is the ability to merge and evolve these cards. By combining duplicate or compatible cards, you can increase their potency, add secondary effects, and eventually transform them into legendary skills.

| Mechanic | Description | Strategic Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Card Merging** | Combine two identical cards to increase level and damage | **High** |
| **Card Evolution** | Transform max-level cards into new, powerful forms | **Critical** |
| **Horde Survival** | Maintain positioning while cards auto-trigger or activate | **Medium** |
| **Ally Support** | Utilize Virgil or Ovid's unique buffs during combat | **High** |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: Merging Timing">
Do not merge your cards immediately if you are surrounded. Sometimes having two separate low-level attacks provides better coverage than one high-level attack with a slower cooldown. Wait for a brief window of safety before upgrading.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Demo Walkthrough">

Navigating the demo requires a balance between exploration and efficient combat. Follow these steps to ensure you reach the final encounter with Queen Minos with a deck capable of victory.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Descent and Basic Deck">
    Start by clearing the initial waves in the Limbo layer. Focus on gathering at least three different attack cards. Avoid taking damage early, as health pickups are rare in the first five minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mastering the Merge">
    Once you collect your first duplicate card, use the merge system. Prioritize upgrading your primary projectile attack. This will allow you to pierce through multiple enemies, which is essential for the upcoming "Lust" layer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Meeting Virgil">
    Around the mid-point of the demo, you will encounter Virgil. He will offer a choice of "Legendary Boons." Select the one that complements your highest-level card to maximize damage output.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Trial of Queen Minos">
    The demo culminates in a battle against Queen Minos. Stay mobile to avoid her "Fiery Judgment" ground attacks. Focus your fire on her while using AoE cards to keep her summoned minions at bay.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Boss Warning">
Queen Minos has a phase-change at 50% health. She will increase her attack speed significantly. Ensure you have at least one defensive or movement-speed card active before this phase begins.
</Callout>

</Section>
