<Section icon={Target} title="Circle Walkthrough: Limbo and Lust">

Progressing through the game requires clearing specific "Circles" of Hell. Currently, Early Access features **Circle 1: Limbo** and **Circle 2: Lust**. Each Circle has unique enemy families, environmental hazards, and a final boss that tests the strength of your build.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Survive the Limbo Wave">
    Focus on filling your four Weapon slots within the first 5 minutes. Rescue Horace and Homer early to gain access to the Carpe Diem and Winged Lyric weapons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat Queen Minos">
    At the 12-minute mark, Queen Minos appears. She uses telegraphed area attacks. Move laterally and save your dash for when her projectile circles close in.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock the Winds of Desire">
    Once Minos is defeated, interact with Virgil in the Forum to unlock Circle 2. You will need to spend some Wails of the Abyss to prepare for the increased difficulty.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Conquer the Lust Circle">
    Lust introduces Succubi and Vipers. These enemies move faster and have higher health. Prioritize the Codex Amoris build to keep enemies slowed while you focus on the Malebranche bosses, Scarmiglione and Libicocco.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Boss Clones">
In Circle 2, Libicocco can create clones of himself. Do not get distracted by the copies; focus your high-damage attacks on the main boss while using area-of-effect weapons to thin out the clones and the surrounding horde.
</Callout>

| Circle | Survival Time | Major Boss | Key Threat |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Limbo** | 12 Minutes | Queen Minos | Brotchi Hordes |
| **Lust** | 12 Minutes | Scarmiglione & Libicocco | Elite Vipers |
