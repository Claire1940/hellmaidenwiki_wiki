<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Mechanics: Deck-Building Meets Survival">

The core appeal of Hell Maiden lies in its "Horde Survival Deck-Building" loop. Players do not just pick upgrades randomly; they build a hand of cards that dictate their offensive and defensive capabilities. This adds a layer of strategy often missing from "bullet heaven" games.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Spirit Cards">
    Choose Spirit Cards that provide passive bonuses, status effects, and base stat increases to define your build's archetype.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Weapon and Mod Cards">
    Combine specific Weapon Cards with Mod Cards to alter fire rates, damage types, and projectile behaviors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Rescue Poets of Limbo">
    Locate legendary figures like Homer and Virgil within the levels to unlock ultimate attacks and signature gear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat Circle Bosses">
    Overcome the guardians of each Circle to permanently unlock their signature abilities for use in your own deck.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Synergy Bonus">
Always look for Mod Cards that complement your Weapon's natural strengths. For example, adding "Lividity" status effects to fast-firing weapons can melt high-HP bosses in seconds.
</Callout>
