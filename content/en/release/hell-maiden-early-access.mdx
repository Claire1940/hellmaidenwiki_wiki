<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Guide">

Navigating the early stages of **hell maiden early access** can be overwhelming due to the non-linear nature of some quest lines. Following a structured approach will ensure you don't miss out on permanent upgrades or essential lore fragments that provide context to Dante's journey.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Descent into Limbo">
    Start by speaking with Virgil to understand the basic navigation controls. Your first goal is to find the "Poets of Limbo," who serve as the initial gatekeepers. Focus on learning the parry timing for the minor spirits here.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Securing the Blessing of Horus">
    Before entering the deeper layers of Purgatorio, you must obtain the Blessing of Horus. This requires completing a combat trial that tests your precision. Successfully obtaining this blessing allows you to infuse your weapons with divine light.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigating Purgatorio">
    This zone introduces environmental hazards. Use your flame to light beacons that provide temporary safety zones. Collect "Coyote Shards" from fallen enemies to upgrade your primary weapon at the central forge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Ascent to Paradiso">
    The final challenge of the current Early Access build involves the ascent. Here, enemies are resistant to standard physical attacks. You must rely heavily on your Flame Essence and the synergies developed in previous steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>
