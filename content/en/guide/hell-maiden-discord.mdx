<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Developer Interaction and Bug Reporting">

AstralShift is known for being highly responsive to the community. The **hell maiden discord** features a structured bug reporting system that ensures every technical glitch is logged and addressed. This direct line to the developers is crucial for a game that relies on complex card interactions.

To report a bug effectively, players are encouraged to use the following format in the #bug-reports channel:

| Field | Required Information |
