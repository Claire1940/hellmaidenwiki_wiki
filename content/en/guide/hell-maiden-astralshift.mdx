<Section icon={Shield} title="Surviving the Circles: Combat Tactics">

Survival in **hell maiden astralshift** is a matter of movement and management. As the "Maiden of Hell" or Dante, you are constantly hounded by "The Malice," a creeping force that limits your movement if you stay in one place for too long.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Gathering">
    Start the run by kiting small groups of enemies. Focus on picking up "Soul Fragments" to draw your first hand of cards. Prioritize a movement speed card if available.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deck Consolidation">
    By the ten-minute mark, you should have at least two Merged cards. Stop taking new Basic cards unless they fit your intended evolution path to avoid clogging your hand.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Boss Preparation">
    Before facing Queen Minos or other Circle Guardians, ensure you have a "Single Target" burst card. AoE is great for waves, but bosses have massive health pools that require focused fire.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Stand">
    In the deepest layers, the screen will be filled with projectiles. Use "Defensive Utility" cards like shields or short-range teleports to navigate the bullet hell patterns.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Bosses in this game are not just "bullet sponges." They have distinct phases. For example, Queen Minos utilizes a "Judgment" mechanic where she marks areas of the floor; standing in these areas leads to an instant card discard, severely weakening your offensive capabilities.

| Boss Name | Location | Primary Attack | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Queen Minos** | Circle of Judgment | Gavel Slam / Card Discard | Stay mobile, focus on single-target |
| **The Malice Core** | Deep Abyss | Tentacle Sweep / Darkness | Light-based cards required |
| **Fallen Legion** | Circle of Violence | Shield Charge / Phalanx | Piercing damage is essential |

<Callout type="info" title="Important Note">
The difficulty in hell maiden astralshift scales with the number of "Sins" you equip before a run. While Sins make the game harder, they significantly increase the drop rate of Rare and Epic cards.
</Callout>
