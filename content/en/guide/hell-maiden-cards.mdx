<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapon and Mod Card Classifications">

Not all **hell maiden cards** are created equal. The game utilizes a rarity and type system to help players quickly identify the function of a card. Weapon Cards come in two distinct rarities: **Silver** (Common) and **Gold** (Rare). Gold cards typically offer more complex mechanics or higher base stats, such as Dante's *Dragon's Breath*.

Mod Cards are further categorized into five types, each color-coded for easy identification during fast-paced runs.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Merging and Leveling Process">

One of the most unique aspects of **hell maiden cards** is the merging mechanic. You can upgrade Mod Cards up to Level 3 by combining duplicates. This process is essential for scaling your power to match the increasing difficulty of the Limbo circles.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Duplicates">
    Obtain a duplicate of a Mod Card you already possess from the level-up pool or a chest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Match Levels">
    Ensure the two cards are of the same level. Two Level 1 cards will merge into a Level 2 card.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Visual Evolution">
    Observe the card border: Level 2 cards feature silver detailing, while Level 3 cards boast gold detailing and altered illustrations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Automatic Combination">
    If you move a card to a slot containing its duplicate of the same level, they will instantly merge to save space.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Deck Sorting">
Pressing the **Tab** key allows you to sort your deck. You can move Mod Cards between different Weapon Cards freely while on the level-up screen to optimize your "Spread" card bonuses.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Character Signature Cards & Spells">

In the 2026 meta, choosing your poet is the first step in defining your card strategy. Each character comes with a Signature Weapon Card that cannot be discarded and includes a powerful "Spell" or ultimate attack. These spells trigger a fully animated cutscene and deal massive screen-clearing damage.

**Dante's Strategy:**
Dante focuses on fire and close-range combat. His signature *Blazing Quill* benefits immensely from Attack Size and Projectile Count mods. His spell, *Dante's Inferno*, is best saved for elite encounters where you are surrounded.

**Virgil's Strategy:**
Virgil utilizes the *Wayfinder*, which excels at long-range crowd control. Players using Virgil should focus on Duration and Projectile Speed to keep his swerving anchors active for as long as possible.

<Callout type="success" title="Elite Rewards">
Elite enemies drop chests that provide free Mod Card selections. These do not consume your level-up points, making them the most efficient way to reach Level 3 on your core modifiers.
