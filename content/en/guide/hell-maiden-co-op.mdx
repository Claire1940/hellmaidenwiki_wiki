<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering the Deck-Building Mechanics">

Success in the underworld depends on how you handle your hand. Unlike other survivor games where upgrades are purely random, Hell Maiden introduces a **Deck-Building** system that requires spatial awareness. Your cards aren't just stats; their position relative to one another determines their effectiveness. For example, some Mod cards only provide bonuses to weapons placed to their immediate right or left.

| Card Category | Functionality | Strategic Importance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Weapon Cards** | Primary damage dealers | Determines your attack pattern (Quill, Harpoon, Sword) |
| **Spirit Cards** | Passive stat boosters | Critical for scaling health, XP gain, and movement speed |
| **Mod Cards** | Weapon enhancements | Must be placed adjacent to weapons to trigger specific buffs |
| **Ultimate Cards** | Screen-clearing abilities | High-cooldown moves like **Stellar Skies** or **Inferno** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Placement Matters">
Always check the description of your Mod cards. Many "Surge" cards only affect **adjacent weapons**. Placing a Mod card in the wrong slot can result in zero benefit for your primary damage source.
</Callout>

The synergy between weapons like the **Quill** and mods that increase **Projectile Count** is essential for surviving the later stages of Limbo. As you progress, you will unlock "Virgil's Blessing," which introduces new card tiers and more complex interaction patterns that can turn a struggling run into a powerhouse build.

<Section icon={Target} title="Character Progression and Rescuing Poets">

The narrative of Hell Maiden is driven by your interactions with the **Poets of Limbo**. These legendary figures from antiquity are scattered across the circles of hell. Rescuing them is not just a story beat; it is the primary way to expand your arsenal and unlock the game's most powerful perks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Poet">
    Explore the map to find the golden icon representing a Poet like **Horace** or **Homer**. They are often guarded by elite enemies or traps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Protection Phase">
    Once you reach a Poet, you must survive a timed wave of enemies while staying within their vicinity. This tests your AoE damage capabilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Blessings">
    Upon a successful rescue, the Poet returns to the Forum, unlocking **5 new abilities** and a signature weapon for your future runs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade Ultimates">
    Rescuing multiple Poets allows you to combine their spirits, leading to advanced ultimate attacks that utilize different elemental damage types.
  </Step>
</Steps>
