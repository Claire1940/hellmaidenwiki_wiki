<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Card Merging and Meta-Deck Strategies">

A key component of **hell maiden save progress** is mastering the card system. While the cards themselves reset each run, your knowledge of which combinations work best allows for faster progression in later stages. The game features five distinct mod card types that can be evolved up to Level 3 through merging.

| Mod Type | Primary Effect | Strategy Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Boost** | Stats increase | Direct weapon power |
| **Effect** | Status ailments | Burn, Slow, Poison |
| **Spread** | Multi-weapon buffs | Synergy builds |
| **Miracle** | Multiple powerful boosts | Rare, high-impact |
| **Curse** | Strong buff / Heavy cost | Risk vs Reward |

Merging requires two cards of the same level and type. For example, dragging a Level 1 "Attack Size" mod onto another Level 1 "Attack Size" mod creates a Level 2 version with enhanced visuals and stats. This is the only way to scale your damage effectively against late-game bosses.

<Callout type="info" title="Merge Mechanics">
You can only modify your deck during the level-up screen. Use this time to swap mod cards between different weapons to optimize your "Signature" ability.
</Callout>
