<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Gameplay Mechanics and Card Evolution">

Survival in the underworld requires more than quick reflexes. Players must master the deckbuilding mechanics to construct a reliable arsenal of attacks, defenses, and utility spells. As you defeat enemies, you collect resources and new cards that can be added to your active hand. The key to overcoming the escalating difficulty lies in the card evolution system, which allows players to combine identical cards to unlock enhanced versions with superior statistics and unique bonus effects.

Positioning is equally critical. The game utilizes a top-down perspective where players must constantly maneuver around obstacles and avoid being cornered by aggressive enemy waves. By combining smart movement with optimized card triggers, you can create devastating defensive perimeters or high-damage sweeps to clear out the demonic forces.
