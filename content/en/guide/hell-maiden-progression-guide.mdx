You can hold up to four unique cards per turn. Effectively swapping and discarding cards allows you to adapt to the increasing difficulty of enemy waves. Always look for opportunities to merge cards of the same level to unlock superior base stats and powerful new effects.

| Mod Type | Benefit Provided | Tactical Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Boost** | Improves single stat | Use on primary weapons |
| **Effect** | Adds unique status | Apply burn/slow to crowds |
| **Spread** | Distributes effects | Best for multiple weapon slots |
| **Miracle** | Rare, powerful buffs | Save for elite encounters |
| **Curse** | High risk, high reward | Use when desperate for power |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Flow">

Progression in this game is not just about raw damage; it is about managing your resources and map exploration. Follow these steps to improve your run efficiency.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize XP Gain">
    Focus on picking up experience orbs early. Look for mods that grant experience bonuses to level up faster during the early game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Define Your Build">
    Choose a signature weapon and commit to a specific synergy, such as fire damage, critical hits, or projectile count, rather than spreading your upgrades too thin.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Dash">
    Use your dash's invincibility frames to maneuver through tight enemy clusters. This is essential for surviving boss encounters where damage output is high.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Card Merging">
    Regularly check your hand for orange-glowing cards. Merge them immediately to clear space and boost your overall power level.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Strategy">
Balancing your defensive stats—like damage reduction and max HP—with your offensive output is key to surviving the later stages of a run.
</Callout>
