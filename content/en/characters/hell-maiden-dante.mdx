Movement is your best defense. <Section icon={Sparkles} title="Spirit Card Strategy: Weapon and Mod Synergies">

The heart of progression in **hell maiden dante** lies in the Spirit Card system. Your hand consists of four Weapon slots and associated Mod slots. To succeed in the deeper circles like Lust, you must move beyond simply picking random upgrades. A "Focused Damage Build" is currently the most reliable way to clear Early Access content. This involves choosing one core weapon, such as the **Blazing Quill**, and stacking it with three Level 3 Mod cards.

Merging duplicate Mod cards is essential. When you select a Mod you already possess, it merges to increase its level, capping at Level 3. Level 3 Mods provide massive stat jumps that are necessary for the 12-minute mark survival waves. Additionally, pay attention to card positioning; some Mods grant bonuses to adjacent weapons or those placed to their right.

| Mod Type | Level 1 Effect | Level 3 Milestone | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Damage** | +15% Raw Damage | +50% Raw Damage / Crit Chance | **High** |
| **Attack Speed** | +10% Frequency | +35% Frequency / Faster Recharge | **High** |
| **Projectiles** | +1 Projectile | +3 Projectiles / Split Pattern | **Medium** |
| **Duration** | +20% Effect Time | +60% Effect Time / Lingering | **Low** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Build Warning">
Avoid filling all four weapon slots too early. If you spread your Mod upgrades across four weak weapons, you will lack the concentrated DPS needed to defeat Scarmiglione or Libicocco in the Lust circle.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="The Poets of Limbo: Unlocking Dante's Companions">

As Dante descends, she encounters the Poets of Limbo—legendary figures who provide her with new weapons, spells, and permanent perks. Rescuing these characters is the primary objective of the story mode and the fastest way to expand your tactical options. Each rescued Poet adds five new abilities to the permanent card pool, significantly increasing the variety of builds available for future runs.

Interacting with these Poets in the Forum between runs is just as important as rescuing them. Exhausting their dialogue not only provides lore context regarding Dante's lost memories but also tracks toward the "Forum Dweller" and "Novice Poet" achievements.

<Callout type="success" title="Unlock Tip">

<Section icon={Target} title="Circle Walkthrough: Mastering Limbo and Lust">

Each circle in **hell maiden dante** presents a 12-minute survival challenge followed by a major boss encounter. Limbo serves as the introduction, featuring Queen Minos, while Lust ramps up the difficulty with the Malebranche bosses, Scarmiglione and Libicocco. The environment also plays a role; you must explore to find and activate Temples, which provide run-specific buffs and are required for the "Pious Pilgrim" achievement.

| Circle | Final Boss | Elite Threats | Survival Length |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Limbo** | Queen Minos | Ghouls, Skeletons | 12 Minutes |
| **Lust** | Scarmiglione / Libicocco | Elite Vipers, Succubi | 12 Minutes |

Queen Minos uses a predictable cycle of telegraphed area attacks and projectile rings. The key is to stay on the outer edge of the arena and only dash when a projectile lane is completely blocked. In contrast, the Lust bosses are much more aggressive. Scarmiglione will constantly close the distance, forcing you to move laterally. Libicocco can create clones, multiplying the visual clutter and making it harder to track the real boss.

<Callout type="info" title="Boss Preparation">
Before the 12-minute timer hits zero, ensure your signature Poet Spell is fully charged. Use it during the boss's second phase when the arena becomes crowded with summoned adds or overlapping projectiles.
</Callout>

</Section>
