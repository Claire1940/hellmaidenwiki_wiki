<Section icon={Map} title="How to Rescue Horace in Circle 1">

Rescuing the Poets of Limbo is the core progression mechanic in Hell Maiden. Horace, known as the Golden Satirist, is one of the earliest companions you can encounter. Unlike standard upgrades, Poets must be found and assisted during an active run in Circle 1 (Limbo). Successfully rescuing her not only advances the story but also permanently expands your Spirit Card pool with some of the most reliable damage options in the Early Access build.

<Callout type="tip" title="Exploration Strategy">
Always prioritize Temple activation while searching for Horace. The **Pious Pilgrim** achievement requires 12 Temples in one run, and Horace's rescue quest often spawns near these landmarks.
</Callout>

| **Time Window** | Before the 12-minute mark |

| **Achievement** | Golden Satirist |

  <Step num="1" title="Enter Limbo">

  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Rescue Quest">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Strategies: Limbo to Lust">

Using Horace's kit effectively requires understanding the transition between the two available Circles. In Limbo, the threats are mostly melee-focused (Imps, Skeletons). In Lust, you face faster, more aggressive enemies like **Vipers** and **Succubi**.

| Enemy Type | Horace Counter Strategy | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Imp / Skeleton** | Standard Carpe Diem fire | Low |
| **Elite Viper** | Focused damage + Spell burst | High |
| **Elite Succubus** | Lateral movement + Projectile spam | High |
| **Queen Minos** | Save dash for telegraphed AOE | Boss |
| **Scarmiglione** | Use Horace's Spell during recovery | Boss |

<Callout type="tip" title="Advanced Positioning">
In Circle 2: Lust, always keep an open escape lane. Horace's weapons are powerful but require precise aiming. If you get surrounded, you lose the ability to focus fire on priority targets like the **Elite Viper**.
</Callout>

When moving toward the **Hellish Mastery II** achievement, remember that Horace's abilities scale well with **Charms of Crystal rarity**. Obtaining these rare items (Heavenly Gift achievement) provides the stat padding necessary to survive the 12-minute mark in Lust, where the enemy density peaks.

</Section>
