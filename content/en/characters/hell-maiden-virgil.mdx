<Section icon={Swords} title="Virgil’s Signature Weapon: The Wayfinder">

The **Wayfinder** is the cornerstone of any **hell maiden virgil** build. It functions as a multi-shot projectile weapon that automatically seeks out targets within a specific radius. While its base damage might seem lower than the Blazing Quill, its utility comes from its fire rate and the ability to stack multiple projectiles. In a horde survival game where you are often surrounded by hundreds of enemies, the Wayfinder provides the "bullet heaven" experience necessary to survive the final minutes of a run.

When building around the Wayfinder, your positioning in the hand matters. Position-based Mod Cards can significantly amplify the Wayfinder's effectiveness if placed correctly. For example, placing a "Projectile Count" mod to the immediate left or right of the Wayfinder card can trigger adjacent bonuses that double your output without requiring extra energy.

<Callout type="tip" title="Positioning Secret">
Always check if your Mod Cards have the **Adjacent Bonus** tag. Virgil's builds thrive when you cluster projectile-enhancing mods around the Wayfinder in the center of your four-weapon hand.
</Callout>

| Mod Priority | Effect | Recommended Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Projectile Count** | Adds +1/2/3 projectiles | Level 3 (Essential) |
| **Attack Speed** | Reduces cooldown between shots | Level 2+ |
| **Attack Duration** | Increases how long projectiles persist | Level 2 |
| **Status Effects** | Adds Burn or Chill to hits | Level 1 (Utility) |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mastering the Star Sign ☆ Pleiades Spell">

Virgil's signature spell, **Star Sign ☆ Pleiades**, is one of the most visually striking and mechanically powerful ultimates in Hell Maiden. When activated, it summons a celestial pattern of projectiles that sweep across the screen, damaging everything in their path. This spell is specifically designed to handle "restrictive patterns"—moments where Queen Minos or the Malebranche bosses force you into a corner.

Unlike standard weapons, the spell has a long cooldown and should be treated as a defensive reset. If you find yourself surrounded by the **Lust Sinner** or **Devi-Brotchi** enemy families in Circle 2, activating Pleiades will create a safe lane for you to dash through. It is also the key to earning the **Wayfinder II** achievement, which simply requires you to trigger the spell during a live run.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Building the Ultimate Virgil Loadout">

To achieve **Hellish Mastery** in either Limbo or Lust using Virgil's kit, you need a structured approach to your 12-minute run. Follow these steps to ensure your deck is optimized before the final boss encounter.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Wayfinder Early">
    Your first level-up should focus on picking the Wayfinder Weapon Card. If it doesn't appear, use a reroll. Starting with your core weapon allows you to begin collecting its specific Mod Cards immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Fill the Weapon Hand">
    By the 4-minute mark, you should have a full hand of four weapons. For a Virgil-focused run, consider adding **Carpe Diem** (Horace) or **Winged Lyric** (Homer) to complement your projectile pressure with single-target damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Merge to Level 3">
    Focus all your upgrade choices on one or two specific Mod Cards. A Level 3 **Projectile Count** mod is significantly more powerful than three Level 1 mods spread across different weapons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate 12 Temples">
    While moving between waves, prioritize visiting Temples. Activating 12 different Temples in a single run unlocks the **Pious Pilgrim** achievement and provides massive stat boosts to your projectiles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Progression Milestone">
Reaching Level 50 during a Virgil run will unlock the **Infernal Prowess I** achievement. This is easiest to do in Circle 2, Lust, where the enemy density provides more experience orbs.
</Callout>
