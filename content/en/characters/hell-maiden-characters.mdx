<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Evolving Cards and Character Synergy">

Surviving the deep layers of Hell requires more than basic attacks. Players must merge duplicate or compatible cards to create evolved forms. This process increases damage, adds status effects, and reduces resource costs, making your deck efficient enough to handle late-game challenges.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Duplicates">
    Gather identical cards during combat waves or purchase them from the river merchant.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access Compilation">
    Open the deck compilation menu between combat rounds to view eligible combinations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Combine Cards">
    Merge the compatible cards to unlock their evolved, higher-tier variants.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip Upgrades">
    Equip the newly evolved cards to your active combat slots for immediate use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Target} title="Boss Encounters: Defeating Queen Minos">

As the fiery judge of Hell, Queen Minos represents the first major roadblock for players. Her arena restricts movement, forcing you to balance dodging fire hazards while dealing consistent damage. Defeating her requires a deck that features high mobility and quick burst damage.

To defeat Queen Minos, you must learn to navigate her distinct combat phases. Each phase introduces new environmental hazards that can quickly end a run if you are caught out of position.

| Phase | Danger Level | Primary Attack | Strategy |
| Phase 1 | Moderate | Whip strike | Dodge laterally |
| Phase 2 | High | Lava geysers | Move to safe tiles |
| Phase 3 | Extreme | Hellfire rain | Keep moving constantly |

<Callout type="warning" title="Boss Warning">
Do not stand in the center of the arena during Phase 3. Queen Minos targets this area with high-damage fire columns.
</Callout>

</Section>
