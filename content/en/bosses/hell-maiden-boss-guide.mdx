### Mod Card Synergies

Mod cards can be merged up to Level 3, significantly increasing their effectiveness. For boss fights, prioritize the following combinations:

1.  **Projectile Count + Wayfinder**: Triples your damage output by firing multiple swirling projectiles.
2.  **Attack Size + Kindle Embers**: Creates a massive "Garlic-style" zone that protects you from smaller projectiles.
3.  **Vampirism (8% Hit Chance)**: Essential for long boss fights to recover HP without relying on rare flask drops.
4.  **Elite Slayer**: A specialized mod that increases damage against bosses by up to 30%.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Boss Strategy">

Following a structured approach during the encounter will ensure you don't get overwhelmed by the visual clutter of the late-game stages.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Positioning">
    Enter the boss arena and immediately move to the outer edge. This gives you more room to see incoming projectiles and plan your movement path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Resource Management">
    Do not use your Q (Ultimate) immediately. Save it for when the boss summons the "Red Eyeball" swarm, which can easily trap you in a corner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Dash Window">
    Only dash when an attack is about to hit you. With only 2 charges, wasting them on general movement can leave you vulnerable during a "Confess" blast.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Healing and Kiting">
    If your HP is low, focus on kiting. If you have the Vampirism mod, your Whirling Spheres will passively heal you as they strike nearby minions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Advice">
The environment can be your enemy. Be careful not to get stuck behind pillars or trees in the Limbo arena, as the hitbox for Dante includes her entire character model.
</Callout>
