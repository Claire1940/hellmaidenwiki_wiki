<Section icon={Swords} title="Strategies to Defeat Hell Maiden Scarmiglione">

The encounter with **hell maiden scarmiglione** is a masterclass in repositioning. Unlike Queen Minos, who relies on telegraphed bullet-hell patterns, Scarmiglione is a "Malebranche" class boss that applies constant, direct pressure on Dante. He is designed to punish players who panic-dash or move predictably.

Scarmiglione often appears alongside Libicocco in the final stages of the Lust encounter. This duo forces you to manage both aggressive melee-style rushes and deceptive clone-based projectile attacks. Understanding the recovery windows after their telegraphed moves is the only way to deal consistent damage without depleting your health pool.

| Attack Name | Description | Recommended Counter |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Aggressive Rush** | Fast closing speed toward player | Move laterally (sideways), do not retreat straight |
| **Malebranche Strike** | High-damage melee swipe | Dash through the boss just as the telegraph flashes |
| **Lustful Pressure** | Summoning Lust Sinners | Use area-of-effect Spells or Spells like Dante's Inferno |
| **Clone Phase** | Libicocco creates multiple copies | Identify the main body and use wide-area projectiles |

<Callout type="warning" title="Boss Synergy">
When fighting the Scarmiglione and Libicocco pair, always clear the summoned clones first. Clones reduce your available escape lanes, making Scarmiglione’s rushes nearly impossible to dodge.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Surviving the Lust Circle">

Surviving the 12 minutes leading up to the boss is often harder than the boss itself due to the density of the Devi-Brotchi and Lust Sinner waves. Follow this progression to ensure you reach the final encounter with full health and a ready Spell.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Deck Stabilization">
    Focus on filling all four Weapon slots in the first 3 minutes. Prioritize one "Main" weapon (like Blazing Quill) and attach at least one Mod to it immediately to increase early-game efficiency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Temple Exploration">
    While the waves are still manageable (minutes 4-7), explore the Lust Circle to activate Temples. Aim to activate at least 6-8 Temples to gain permanent run bonuses and progress toward the Pious Pilgrim achievement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mod Merging & Leveling">
    From minute 8 onward, stop picking new weapons. Use every level-up to find duplicate Mod Cards. Merge them to reach Level 3 on your primary damage weapon. This is critical for the "Boss-Focused" phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Minute Prep">
    At the 11-minute mark, stop taking long detours. Clear a large area of Lust Sinners to ensure you have a clean "arena" for the boss spawn. Ensure your Poet Spell (like Virgil's Star Sign) is fully charged.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Resource Management">
Don't forget to spend your **Wails of the Abyss** between runs. Upgrading your base health and dash cooldown in the meta-progression menu is essential for surviving the high-speed attacks of the Malebranche bosses.
</Callout>

</Section>
