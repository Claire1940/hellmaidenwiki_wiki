<Section icon={Target} title="How to Progress in Hell Maiden">

Whether you are playing on PC now or waiting for **Hell Maiden on Xbox**, the core progression remains the same. You must follow Dante's path, guided by Virgil, to rescue the Poets of Limbo who act as your primary source of power-ups.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Loadout">
    Choose one of the 4 signature weapons. Each weapon has a distinct playstyle, ranging from rapid-fire projectiles to heavy-hitting melee strikes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Rescue Poets of Limbo">
    Navigate the current Circles of Hell to find legendary poets like Homer or Ovid. Rescuing them unlocks 5 new abilities per poet and permanent perks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Card Merging">
    During a run, collect duplicate Spirit Cards. Merging them increases their tier, providing massive stat boosts necessary for defeating Bosses like Queen Minos.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Conquer the Bosses">
    Defeat the guardian of each Circle. Each defeated boss grants 2 unique abilities that are added to your permanent card pool for future runs.
  </Step>
</Steps>
