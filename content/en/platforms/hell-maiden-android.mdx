<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies for the 9 Circles">

Surviving the hordes in **hell maiden android** requires more than just good cards; it requires tactical positioning and resource management. Each of the two currently available unique levels (themed around the Circles of Hell) presents distinct enemy types and boss battles.

You must adapt your hand as you go, refining your personal playstyle to counter the specific fiends of each circle. The game encourages "editing and reformulating" your hand mid-run, allowing for a level of flexibility rarely seen in the horde survival genre.

| Combat Aspect | Recommended Tactic | Expected Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Horde Control** | Use AOE Weapon Cards with Frost Mods | Slows down enemies, prevents swarming |
| **Boss Battles** | Focus on Single-Target Boss Abilities | High burst damage to bypass phases |
| **Resource Gain** | Prioritize Spirit Cards with Loot Buffs | Faster deck completion and upgrades |

**Key Survival Milestones:**
- **Survive the first 5 minutes**: Focus on establishing a core weapon and at least one Mod Card.
- **Defeat the Mid-Boss**: This usually grants 2 new abilities that can define your late-game strategy.
- **Reach the Circle Exit**: Ensure your deck has enough defensive Spirit Cards to survive the final rush.

<Callout type="info" title="Adaptive Combat">
