<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Gameplay: Deckbuilding and Card Evolution">

Survival in the abyss requires more than quick reflexes; it demands strategic foresight. The gameplay loop centers on drafting cards that represent different combat actions, spells, and defensive maneuvers. As you defeat enemies, you gather resources to purchase new cards or upgrade existing ones.

The defining mechanic of the game is card evolution. By merging identical or complementary cards, you unlock enhanced versions with unique modifiers and increased damage potential.

| Card Type | Base Effect | Evolved Effect | Synergy Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Attack** | Direct physical damage | Multi-target slash with bleed | High attack speed builds |
| **Spell** | Ranged elemental projectile | Area-of-effect elemental explosion | Spell power and cooldown reduction |
| **Defense** | Temporary shield barrier | Shield with reflective spike damage | Retaliation and tank builds |
| **Utility** | Movement speed boost | Dash leaving a trail of fire | High mobility and positioning |

  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Card Merging">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Complementary Relics">
