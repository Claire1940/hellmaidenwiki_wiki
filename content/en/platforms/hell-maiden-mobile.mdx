Understanding how to layer these card types is the key to creating overpowered builds that can clear screen-filling waves of enemies in seconds.

<Section icon={Shield} title="How to Play Hell Maiden Mobile via Streaming">

While the game is currently developed as an Early Access title on Steam, players can easily enjoy **hell maiden mobile** on iOS and Android devices using modern streaming technology. Because the game features intuitive directional movement and automated firing mechanics, it translates perfectly to touchscreens and mobile gamepads.

Follow these steps to configure your mobile device for optimal portable play.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Configure Steam Link or Moonlight">
    Install the Steam Link app on your mobile device. Ensure your host PC and mobile device are connected to the same local network, or use a high-speed 5G connection for remote play. Pair the devices using the secure authorization code.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Control Layout">
    Once connected, enter the Steam Link controller settings. Choose a touch control layout that places the virtual analog stick on the left and utility buttons (such as ultimate attacks and card swapping) on the right.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Adjust Video and Bandwidth Settings">
    To minimize input lag, set the video streaming quality to "Fast" or "Balanced" within the app settings. Lock the stream resolution to 1080p at 60 frames per second to ensure smooth, responsive gameplay during dense horde waves.
  </Step>
</Steps>
