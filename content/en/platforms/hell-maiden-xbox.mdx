At its core, the gameplay challenges players to survive waves of demonic entities while building an optimized deck of offensive and defensive abilities. Instead of traditional turn-based card play, action occurs in real-time. Players must move defensively while their card deck dictates their auto-attacks, defensive shields, and utility spells.
