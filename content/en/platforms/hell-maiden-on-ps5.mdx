<Section icon={Target} title="Navigating the 9 Circles of Hell">

To reach salvation and find answers in Paradiso, Dante must descend through all 9 Circles of Hell. Each circle presents unique environmental hazards and enemy archetypes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Descent Begins">
    Start in the upper circles (Limbo and Lust) to build your initial deck. Focus on high-frequency attacks to manage the initial weak swarms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Rescue the Poets">
    Locate trapped Poets of Limbo like Homer and Ovid. Freeing them grants access to legendary perks and new abilities that are permanent for the rest of the run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Confront the Judges">
    Every two circles culminate in a massive boss fight. Defeating a boss like Queen Minos unlocks their signature abilities for your own deck.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master the Deck">
    By the time you reach the deeper circles (Malebolge or Cocytus), your deck must be optimized. Utilize "Ultimate Attacks" earned from your allies to survive the final waves.
  </Step>
</Steps>
