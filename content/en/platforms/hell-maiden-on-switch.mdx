<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Gameplay & Deck-Building Mechanics">

The core loop of the game revolves around surviving endless waves of demonic forces while building a powerful deck of abilities. Unlike traditional bullet heaven games, your offensive capabilities are directly tied to how you construct, modify, and manage your hand during a run.

The deck-building system is divided into three distinct card categories, each serving a specific tactical purpose. Understanding how these cards interact is the key to conquering the deeper circles of Hell.
